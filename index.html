Hawk Tower: isolation, together.

Trees connect many different animals together, and they can even connect a community in quarantine. This tree is made up of RWU hawk eyries, created by students at RWU. Inspired by Corona Maison and Tunnel66 Hawk Tower is a place to gather and showcase the unique identities of the RWU community, in lieu of physically being on campus at RWU. Each hawk adds an eyrie, forming one connected tower towards the sky. Click on a eyrie to learn more about the artist!
